Who is a Student Ambassador?

A student ambassador is someone who acts as an ambassador between Yugam and a different institution or university. The role of a student ambassador is to make sure that everyone in his or her college or university knows that a fest is happening like this and make sure that whoever is interested participates in the events or workshops.

The student ambassadors will be the point of contact for all participants from their college or university and they will also help in marketing the events through social media and through their entire study.

The student ambassadors will enjoy benefits because they will be super stars if they make our family larger!
