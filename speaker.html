About the Event

The future belongs to those who dream and to those who believe in it. Acquiring insights from the previous editions of Y Talks, it is evident that the impact is limitless as the ripple perpetually dilates. From a talk series to a conclave, is the leap that is needed and rational as it broadens the scope for the exchange of ideas, networking, and discussions. The more the youth dreams, the better the nation becomes. In light of this, we launch to you the Inspire India YouthCon 2019.

What’s Inspire India YouthCon about?

Inspire India YouthCon 2019, a 2 day conclave aimed to reach out to the young minds across the nation for a learning culmination. The conclave will be a place where icons inspire and seekers aspire to be a part of the India’s growth story and build the Nation we dream.

Spotlight

30+Speakers, 3+Workshops, 10+Conversations, 5+Young Disruptor stories, 3+Leadership Impact Talks, 2+Proshow and more.

What can you expect?

Icons and young disruptors from all parts of the Nation as well as the world will confluence to meet the 600 young minds.Inspire India YouthCon will be a place to network, learn, ideate, unlearn, aspire, question and experience two days of euphoria.
